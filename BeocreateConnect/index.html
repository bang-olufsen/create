Find & Set Up

Turn on and configure the product for the first time.

Choose destination

Connect your MicroSD card (4 GB or higher capacity) to the computer. When the card appears below, select it to continue.

Writing card...

About 3 minutes remaining

Insert the card

Carefully insert the prepared MicroSD card to the slot on the Raspberry Pi.

Don't close up your speaker just yet, until you've made sure everything is working.

Power on your product

Use a compatible, 12-24 V power adapter. Connect it either to the barrel jack or screw terminals, making sure polarity is correct.

A red light should be seen on the Raspberry Pi. If not, check connections and try again.

Find your product

Connect this computer to a Wi-Fi hotspot that looks like Beocreate_Setup_xxxxxxx. It may take a few minutes for the hotspot to appear. Set up with Ethernet instead.

Connect the product to your network with an Ethernet cable. Make sure this computer is on the same network. Set up wirelessly instead.

Once connected and the product appears below, select it to continue.
